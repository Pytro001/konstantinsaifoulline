History

Origins

Born in Germany, growing up in a small town called Dresden, I developed my first musical skills playing instruments and Lego all day long. One of my biggest advantages are my always caring mother and father that shaped my first years growing up.

Pytro

At the age of 15, I stepped into the public eye as an artist and creator under the name Pytro. In just 6 months I was able to play 30 shows in clubs in Germany and release remixes that hit up to 1M streams on SoundCloud.

Playing at #7 Club in the World

At 16 years, I released 'Somebody to Love' with MATTN and Klaas, a remix that hit 100,000 streams on Spotify in one day. One day later I played my first show at Bootshaus Cologne, the #7 best club in the world.

First Golden Plate

After posting some remixes on TikTok in 2023, 'Blonde Chaya' with Niklas Dee went viral, giving me Gold status with over 100M streams on one song in collaboration with Universal Music. While still finishing high school, I played regular shows across Europe, collaborating with influencers and DJs and hosting my own events.

HUGE Production GmbH

I also started a podcast with Tade Mehl about entrepreneurship. After only half a year, we traveled to Dubai, London, and Paris to meet our guests and learn how to build great products. The HUGE Conversations Show was incorporated in Germany under the name HUGE Productions.

Travel and Learn

I spent time around acknowledged people in engineering and science in San Francisco, California and Shenzhen, China and reading a lot of books.

Walking

In 2026 I started my second company, this time to fulfill my vision of achieving a great future for humanity through physics-based engineering.
